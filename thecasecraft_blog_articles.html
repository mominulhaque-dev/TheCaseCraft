As TPU breaks down from UV exposure, it becomes more rigid and brittle. Rigid TPU absorbs less impact energy. A yellowed case protects your phone less effectively than it did when it was clear. If your case has gone from clear to yellow: replace it. The material degradation is real. SIGN 5: BUTTON FEEDBACK HAS CHANGED The side buttons on your iPhone should feel identical whether you're pressing through your case or without it. If the tactile feedback has softened, become mushy, or the buttons feel like they're "traveling" more than usual — the case material over the buttons has worn down. While this is primarily an annoyance, it's also a durability indicator. Cases show wear at the buttons first. If buttons are degraded, other areas of the case are close behind.
